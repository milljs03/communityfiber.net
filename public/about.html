Connecting Neighbors Since 1901

From the first telephone lines to gigabit fiber, we've been Northern Indiana's local connection for 125 years.

More Than Just Internet

In an age of faceless corporate giants, we remain proudly owned by local shareholders. We live in the communities we serve, which means your connection is our connection. We don't just build networks; we build relationships that stand the test of time.

Our Heritage: NPTech

New Paris Telephone was formed in 1901 with a simple mission: to bring telephone service to our rural community. For over a century, we have adapted to the ever-changing landscape of technology.

In 2022, we rebranded as NPTech to better reflect our evolution. While we honor our roots in traditional telephony, we are now a full-service technology provider offering:

  • Computer Repair & IT Support
  • IP Telephone Systems
  • Enterprise Cabling (Cat6)
  • Cable TV Services

Our Future: Community Fiber

Community Fiber Network (formerly Goshen Fiber Network) represents our commitment to the future. As a division of New Paris Telephone, we combine 125 years of stability with cutting-edge fiber infrastructure.

We changed our name to reflect our expansion beyond Goshen. We are actively building and connecting schools, businesses, and homes across Northern Indiana, including:

Goshen New Paris Milford Middlebury Nappanee Wakarusa Elkhart

And we're just getting started.
