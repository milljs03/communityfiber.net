Test Your Internet Speed

Verify your connection performance. For the most accurate results, please close other tabs.

Tips for Accurate Results

  • Use a wired Ethernet connection if possible for Gigabit testing.
  • Close other apps (Steam, Zoom, etc.) that might be using bandwidth.
  • Ensure no other devices are streaming 4K video or downloading large files.
  • If testing on WiFi, stand close to your router (5GHz band recommended).
